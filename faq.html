Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our grocery delivery service.

Ordering

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, click "Add to Cart" on items you want, and proceed to checkout. You can review your cart anytime by clicking the cart icon in the header. Once you're ready, click "Proceed to Checkout" to complete your order.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify your order up to 30 minutes before the scheduled delivery time. Contact our support team at support@todayshop.com or call +91 98765 43210 for assistance with modifications.

Is there a minimum order value?

No, we don't have a minimum order value. You can order any amount, and we'll deliver it to your doorstep. However, orders below ₹500 will have a nominal delivery fee of ₹50.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your order in real-time through our mobile app or by logging into your account on our website.

Delivery

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery is completely free on orders above ₹500. A nominal fee of ₹50 is charged for orders below ₹500. We also offer express delivery for an additional ₹100 if you need your order within 20 minutes.

How long does delivery take?

We deliver within 30-60 minutes for standard orders. During peak hours (6 PM - 9 PM), delivery may take up to 90 minutes. Express delivery is available for orders that need to be delivered within 20 minutes.

Do you deliver on Sundays and holidays?

Yes, we deliver 7 days a week including Sundays and all major holidays. Delivery timings on Sunday are 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. On other days, we deliver from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all major cities across India including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, Kolkata, and Ahmedabad. We're expanding to more cities every month!

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, Amazon Pay), Net Banking (all major banks), and Cash on Delivery. We also accept Sodexo and other meal vouchers.

Is it safe to pay online?

Absolutely! All transactions are encrypted and secure using 256-bit SSL technology. We never store your payment information on our servers. We are PCI-DSS compliant, ensuring your payment data is always protected.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery for all orders. Our delivery partners will collect the payment when they deliver your order. Please keep the exact change ready for a smooth transaction.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with any product, you can return it within 24 hours of delivery. Simply contact our support team and we'll arrange a pickup. Perishable items must be returned within 2 hours of delivery.

How long does it take to get a refund?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned items. The amount will be credited to your original payment method. For Cash on Delivery orders, we'll send a bank transfer or store credit.

What if I receive a damaged or wrong product?

In the rare event that you receive a damaged or wrong product, please contact us immediately. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund at no extra cost. Please keep the product packaging for inspection.

Account

How do I create an account?

Click on the person icon in the header and select "Sign Up". Fill in your details including name, email, phone number, and address. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account. Once verified, you're ready to start shopping!

How do I reset my password?

Click on the person icon, select "Forgot Password", enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. You'll receive a password reset link that expires in 24 hours.

How can I change my delivery address?

Log in to your account, go to "My Profile" or "Account Settings", and click on "Addresses". You can add, edit, or delete delivery addresses from there. Make sure to set your default address correctly.

How do I view my order history?

Log in to your account and click on "My Orders" from the dashboard. You'll see a complete list of all your past orders with their status, total amount, and delivery details. You can also reorder items from your history.
