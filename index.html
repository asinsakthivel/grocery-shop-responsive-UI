Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our grocery delivery service.

Ordering

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, click "Add to Cart" on items you want, and proceed to checkout. You can review your cart anytime by clicking the cart icon in the header.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Yes, up to 30 minutes before delivery. Contact support@todayshop.com or call +91 98765 43210.

Is there a minimum order value?

No minimum. Orders below ₹500 have a ₹50 delivery fee.

Delivery

What are the delivery charges?

Free above ₹500. ₹50 below ₹500. Express ₹100 for 20-min delivery.

How long does delivery take?

30-60 min standard, up to 90 min peak. Express 20 min.

Do you deliver on Sundays?

Yes, 7 days a week. Sunday hours 9 AM – 9 PM.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

Cards, UPI, Net Banking, Cash on Delivery, Sodexo.

Is it safe to pay online?

Absolutely. 256-bit SSL encryption, PCI-DSS compliant.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

100% satisfaction. Return within 24h, perishables within 2h.

How long does a refund take?

3-5 business days after return.
